About Us

AARK Engineering, a dynamic team of experienced engineers, specializes in Turnkey Electrical Contracts, annual maintenance, and MSME UDYAM-certified services. We design and manufacture Industrial & Commercial EB Panel Boards, LT, HT & MV Panels, PDB, APFC, AMF, phase sequence correctors, PLC & Drive control panels. Our expertise extends to the design and manufacturing of Industrial Tubular Heaters. Committed to quality, safety, and adherence to EB norms, we supply electrical materials and complete installations promptly. Our advanced, innovative, and economical electrical system designs accommodate future expansion needs. Equipped with state-of-the-art testing tools, we ensure efficient power systems and offer seminars on safety and maintenance. AARK Engineering, a registered Electrical Contractor, is your partner for comprehensive electrical solutions with alliances with leading OEMs in India.
